Photography

I try to capture a sense of atmosphere in my photography. I often work with shadows, grit, and grain to create images that feel lived in and authentic. Rather than simply documenting a place, my goal is to capture the feeling a space evokes and the mood it carries.

Much of my photographic style is developed through post-production, which allows me to translate my artistic vision from my imagination into the final image. It is an important part of my creative process and helps me shape each photograph into something that reflects how I experienced the moment.
